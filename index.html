Matricready

Our Mission

To improve the overall academic infrastructure in Africa by providing a transparent and secure channel through which educational outcomes can be measured.

Our Story

The need for educational reform in Africa is one that has long been characterized by a lack of resources, teacher training, quality curricula, and technological infrastructure. It is also one that is near to the hearts of our founders who have personally experienced educational inadequacies within West Africa’s regions and have made it their mission to inspire this change through technology.

MATRICREADY: Transcripts you can trust at the click of a button!

Matric Ready provides a trusted, unified, and secured ecosystem that delivers relevant information to each respective stakeholder across the educational value chain – from students, teachers, parents, and administrators. Starting at the primary level, Matric ready tracks the progression of students through primary and secondary and beyond, providing a continuous record of individual success that also serves to optimize the entire education system by delivering accurate, contextual insight into academic performance.

Power of Blockchain

Blockchains are inherently decentralized, which allows us to promote the exchange of valuable data using the fastest, most secure technology available.

It also has several safety protocols that ensure the security and privacy of data while providing transparency around the outcome of records. It has the potential to transform education in Africa by creating a secure channel through which primary and secondary educational outcomes can be measured.

  1. Robust student data management
  2. Long term student performance tracking
  3. Accurate transparent record keeping
